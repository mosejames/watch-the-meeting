# Watch the Meeting

**College Park City Council — March 2, 2026**

An interactive accountability page that documents key moments from the College Park City Council meeting on March 2, 2026. Each finding is timestamped and linked directly to the [official city YouTube video](https://www.youtube.com/live/wupGM588Gnk).

## What's Here

- `index.html` — Single-page site with 13 documented findings across 4 tiers
- Each finding card includes context, key quotes, and a clickable video thumbnail that opens YouTube at the exact moment

## Tiers

| Tier | Category | Findings |
|------|----------|----------|
| 1 (Red) | Direct Financial Violations | Funeral payment, gift cards, $10K spending violations, overpayment |
| 2 (Gold) | Structural Power Plays | Mayor restrictions, consent agenda, retroactive approvals, CM search |
| 3 (Navy) | Public Testimony | Procurement testimony, business owner request, short-term rentals |
| Context | Background | $30M debt, staff salaries, bereavement fund, misinformation narrative |

## Deploy

This is a static single-file site. Enable GitHub Pages from `main` branch, root (`/`), and it's live.

## Source

All content sourced from the [City of College Park official YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/live/wupGM588Gnk).
