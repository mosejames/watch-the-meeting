Tier 1 Direct Financial Violations
1A
City Paid ~$3,000 for a Private Funeral Using Public Funds
2:09:53 – 2:38:15
▼
What Happened

A council member discovered a $2,995 payment to "Atlanta Funeral Assistance, LLC" under contractual services. The interim city manager acknowledged authorizing it unilaterally for an employee whose son died. The city attorney confirmed on the record that public funds cannot legally be used for private charitable purposes.

"We are paying for a funeral service, full stop, is an issue... We don't have the authority to do that with public money."
City Attorney — on the record
Why It Matters

Textbook misuse of public funds, confirmed by the city's own attorney in real time. The payment was buried in a line item and never disclosed to council.
