$18,000 retreat approved in 90 minutes while $9.7 million city debt festers and board members trade personal attacks

Source: City of College Park Official YouTube
Meeting Briefing

The March 11 BIDA meeting devolved into familiar College Park dysfunction as Chair Willis pushed through an $18,000 retreat facilitator while the authority owes the city $9.7 million with no formal repayment plan. Board member Jefferson repeatedly called out financial irregularities and questioned Willis's $68,000 Christmas party overspend from 2024. Meanwhile, attorney fees are running triple the budget at $35,000 per quarter. The meeting featured bitter exchanges, questions about missing strategic plans, and a failed vote on requiring highest-and-best-use property appraisals - all while residents' basic concerns about economic development took a back seat to power struggles.
