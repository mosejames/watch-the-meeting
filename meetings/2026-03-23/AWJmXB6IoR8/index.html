Meeting Briefing

What started as routine business turned into a municipal meltdown when Councilman Roderick Gay stormed out mid-meeting, then had his spokesperson read an explosive statement alleging harassment, intimidation, and coordinated action by colleagues. The drama unfolded around a $4.8 million emergency roof repair that City Attorney Winston Denmark blocked for violating Georgia bidding laws — forcing a contentious debate about whether years of delayed maintenance could be called an 'emergency.' Meanwhile, Dr. Shakita James confronted council about her son being used as 'clickbait' in political Facebook posts, and Councilwoman McKenzie accused Mayor Motley Broom of leaking her $15,000 Ward 1 celebration spending to create negative press. The three-hour meeting showcased a city government in complete dysfunction, with officials attacking each other while approving millions in spending with minimal discussion.
