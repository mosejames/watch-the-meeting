Meeting Briefing

The April 6 council meeting made national sports headlines when Councilman Gay announced WNBA superstar Angel Reese — known as 'Bayou Barbie' — is joining College Park's professional team at Gateway Arena. But beneath the celebrity glitz, council quietly rubber-stamped $773,000 in contracts with minimal discussion, including a $348K sewer replacement and $125K water main repair. Meanwhile, residents lined up to praise new City Manager Michael Hicks while warning about expired landscaping contracts and procurement violations. The meeting showcased College Park's strange duality: world-class sports franchises and amateur-hour governance.
